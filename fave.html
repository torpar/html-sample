My Favorite Football Team

I have been a fan of the Minnesota Vikings ever since I was little kid in the Seventies. At that time, they were a Super Bowl contender and went a couple of times, only to lose. I just knew they would get back into the Super Bowl and I liked their Viking mascot, so I picked them. Unfortunately, they did go back to the Super Bowl, only to lose again. For a long time, they were the team with the most Super Bowl losses, a record that has been surpassed by the Buffalo Bills. I am a loyal kind of guy and have stuck with the Vikes through a lot of awful seasons. They are finally becoming an upper tier team again and may be back in the Super Bowl soon. I hope they win this time!
