Welcome to the HTML Examples Site.

This site allows you to see examples of the Five F HTML websites created by former students. The examples range from exemplary to average. You may look at the sties and emulate them if you like. Just don't copy them exactly!

The Assignment

Objective: Student will create a 5 page website housed on their flash drive. Each page will highlight one of the 5 Fs. Because you have a front page or title page, you will not need to use all of the choices below. Choose from the list below:

  • front page (must have this)
  • family (highlight your family)
  • friends (highlight your friends)
  • food (describes your favorite food)
  • favorites (make a page of your favorite things)
  • fun (describe what you do for that)
Each page must contain a navigation scheme that allows users to get to each page at any time. That means the navigation scheme must be present on all pages. The site must be written in HTML and cannot be created from a template like Google Sites. Students will be graded according to a rubric.

Grading

As you look at the samples, ask yourself, "which sample earned a C? Which sample earned an A? Which is in the middle?"
