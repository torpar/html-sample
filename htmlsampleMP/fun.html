For Fun

I like to play soccer for fun. I've played since I was eleven years old. I was never fast or a good ball handler. But, I tried hard. During college, I took a break from playing, then started again in my thirties, playing on an adult team for the next twenty years. I had to have shoulder surgery after playing goalie and broke some ribs running into another player. But, I still had fun.
