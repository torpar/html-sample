My Favorite Food

I love to eat. I have eaten so many scrumtious things in my life. I have also eaten some un-scrumptious foods like ants, crickets, and a lizard tail! I am defintely an omnivore. Of all the things I've tried, the one that I love the most, the one I can't say no too, the one that makes me eat too much, is cookies. I just love them. Here are my favorites in order of preference:

  1. chocolate chip
  2. snickerdoodle
  3. peanut butter
  4. Oreo

Even though those are the best, I will eat most any kind of cookie. They are the scrumpdillyicious!
